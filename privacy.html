Privacy Policy

I respect your privacy. Here's what you need to know.

This Privacy Policy applies to afstevens.com and signalandclarity.com. It explains how Drew Stevens ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

What Information Do We Collect?

If We Send You a Cold Email

When we reach out via cold email, we may collect:
• Your name (if publicly available)
• Your business or work email address

If You Contact Us

When you reach out via our contact form or email us directly, we collect:
• Your name
• Your email address
• Any information you choose to share in your message

How We Use Your Information

Cold Email Outreach
We use your email address to contact you about our services. If your name is publicly available, we may use it to personalise our outreach.

Contact Form Inquiries
We use the information you provide to:
• Respond to your inquiry
• Send you updates about our services or relevant resources (with your consent)
• Add you to our outreach list for future marketing communications (you can opt out at any time)

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for advertising or profiling purposes.

Legal Basis for Processing

We rely on legitimate interest to promote relevant services to professionals or businesses who may benefit from them. You can object to this processing at any time (see "Your Rights" below).

For contact form inquiries, we process your data based on your consent and our legitimate interest in responding to your request.

Third-Party Service Providers

We may use trusted third-party services to help us manage outreach and communications, including:
• Email service providers (e.g., Google Workspace)
• Website hosting and analytics (e.g., Netlify, Google Analytics)

These providers process data on our behalf and are contractually obligated to protect your information. We do not sell or share your data for their own advertising purposes.

Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use cookies and analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors interact with our site. This helps us improve user experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

Data Retention

We retain your contact information for up to 2 years from our last interaction, unless you request deletion or opt out of communications. After this period, your data is automatically deleted from our systems.

Your Rights

Under data protection laws (including GDPR), you have the right to:
• Access the personal data we hold about you
• Correct any inaccurate or outdated information
• Delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
• Object to receiving further emails from us
• Withdraw consent at any time (if we're processing your data based on consent)

How to Exercise Your Rights

To make any of the above requests, simply:
• Reply to our email, or
• Contact us through our website contact form: Contact Form

We'll respond to your request within 30 days.

Questions or Concerns?

We're happy to help. Reach out any time via:
• A reply to our email
• Our website contact form: Contact Form

Updates to This Policy

This policy may be updated periodically. The latest version will always be available on our website.
